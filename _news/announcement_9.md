This summer I'm teaching [_Ethical Vibe Coding_](https://vibe-coding-ethics.tech.cornell.edu/website/) (TECHIE 1121) to high school students as part of Cornell Tech's [Summer Innovation Intensives](https://tech.cornell.edu/programs/summer-innovation-intensives/). Students are building real apps with AI tools while centering human values, probing bias, and reasoning about what "AI for good" really means.
